Biomimicry represents the convergence of natural evolution and technological innovation. Key foundations:
- 3.8 billion years of evolutionary R&D
- Nature's circular economy principles
- Sustainable design patterns
- Energy-efficient biological systems

Modern applications:
- Quantum computing inspired by photosynthesis
- Neural networks modeled on brain architecture
- Self-healing materials based on biological tissue
- Swarm robotics following collective animal behavior
- Nano-materials inspired by gecko feet and lotus leaves
