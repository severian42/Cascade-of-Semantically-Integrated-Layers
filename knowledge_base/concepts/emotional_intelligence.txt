Emotional Intelligence (EI) represents the capacity to process emotional information:

Core Components:
1. Emotional Awareness: Recognition of emotions in self and others
2. Emotional Processing: Understanding emotional causes and effects
3. Emotional Integration: Combining emotional with logical thinking
4. Emotional Regulation: Managing emotional responses

Key Dimensions:
- Self-Awareness
- Social Awareness
- Relationship Management
- Decision Making
- Stress Management

Neural Foundations:
- Amygdala Processing
- Prefrontal Integration
- Limbic System Dynamics
- Mirror Neuron Activity
- Emotional Memory Formation
