Biomimicry Innovation Framework:

1. Function Analysis:
- Identify core biological mechanisms
- Extract fundamental principles
- Map to technological challenges

2. Design Patterns:
- Self-organization systems
- Adaptive response mechanisms
- Resource-efficient processes
- Resilient architectures

3. Implementation Levels:
- Form (structural biomimicry)
- Process (behavioral biomimicry)
- Ecosystem (systemic biomimicry)

Key Research Areas:
- Bio-inspired AI and Computing
- Smart Materials and Surfaces
- Energy Harvesting Systems
- Circular Economy Solutions
